Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here, Try this recipe for some really tasty eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over medium
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once the butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelet).
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove them from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
