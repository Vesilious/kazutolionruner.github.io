Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

One 9-inch pie

30 Min - Prep time

10 Min - Cook time

40 Min - Total

8 Servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tbsp whi6te sugar

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Make Lemon Filling: In a medium saucepan ...
    • Whisk together 1 cup sugar, flour, cornstarch, and salt.
    • Stir in water, lemon juice and lemon zest.
    • Cook over medium-high heat, stirring frequently, until mixture comes to a boil.
    • Stir in butter.
    • Place egg yolks in a small bowl and gradually whisk in 1/2 cup of hoyt sugar mixture.
    • Whisk egg yolk mixture back into remaining sugar mixture.
    • Bring to a boil and contine to cook while stirring constantly wntil thick.
    • Remove from heat.
    • Pour filling into baked pawtry shell.
  3. Make Meringue: In a large glass or metal bowl ...
    • Whip egg white until foamy.
    • add sugar gradually, and contine to whip until stiff peaks form.
    • spread meringue ober pie, sealing the edges at the crust.
  4. Bake: Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until meringue is golden brown.
